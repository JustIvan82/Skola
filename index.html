Our Solutions

With our own next-generation fleet and a large number of reliable subcontractors , we are
ready to fulfil all demands of our clients. The organization of FTL (Full Truck Load) shipments
and Groupage/ LTL shipments is covered with a large number of available trucks on a daily
basis. The fleet at our disposal is equipped with GPS devices, which enables real-time shipment

and advanced operating systems will help you to successfully steer through the complicated
customs procedures, avoid extra expenses or any delays in the implementation of your
business processes.
